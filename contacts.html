FAQ

Let's start with some general question!

Q:  When are fees collected?
A:  Fees for Daycare and Tutoring Services will be collected on a monthly basis at the start of each month and due date is at the end of the second week. Payment of all fees is by cash, cheques and crdit cards. All fees paid are non-refundable, non-transferable and non-negotiable.

Questions about our Daycare Centre?

Q:  What ages of children do you accept to take care of?
A:  Xavier’s Childcare Centre is very happy to welcome for children 8 weeks to 12 years of age and we can provide a safe and educational environment for them!

Q:  Do you provide meals or snacks for children?
A:  Yes! We serve them with hot meals and nutritious snacks to keep the freshness and make sure they eat healthy. Our centre is equipped with a kitchen and staff trained in nutrition. Weekly menu is usually posted on the bulletin board in the lobby. If you child has any food allergies, please inform staff members during registration.

Q:  What do I need to bring up with when sign up for child care?
A:  The registration process will require the following document: Proof of income/Source of Income, Both parents and children’s Identity Card(MYKad) and Authorization for Medical Healthcare.

Q:  What safety procedure are being used at Xavier’s Childcare Centre?
A:  Our facility is cleaned everyday using a safe disenfactant, including toys and equipments, classroom, bathroom and offices to prevent children from geting sick. (#1 Priority:Children Safety)

Q:  What happens if I am delayed at pick up time?
A:  Please contact our staff and notify them as soon as possible, including the estimated arrival time, so that we may handle the situation better.

Q:  Does the daycare centre open on public holidays?
A:  Yes, we usually open on public holidays but the extra fees must be paid.

What about the Tutoring Centre?

Q:  Are the tutors qualified teachers?
A:  The tutors are experts in the fields that they teach. Our tutors also have 3-6 years tutor experienced and they will provide some fun teaching method during the class so students wont feel boring while learning in class!

Q:  Does the Tutoring Centre provide private tutoring?
A:  No,the centre does not offer any private tutoring services but extra classes are conducted during the holidays for the students (not compulsory to join the extra classes).
