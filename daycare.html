Daycare Services

The biggest challenge faced by working parents today is the welfare of their chidren when they are away working.Safe environment and nutritious food is always the priority to make sure them live a healthy life.

At Xavier's, we provide a safe and secure environment for the children to explore freely around the centre and enjoy their time here.

Our friendly and experienced staff will be prepared to assist your child in developing their social skills and help them to find what they interested in. Lastly, We’d be glad that we can be friends with them and able to see them to grow up in the future!
