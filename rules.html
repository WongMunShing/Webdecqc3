Rules and Regulations

General Rules :

  1. Daycare and Tutoring services payment must be paid in the span of the first two weeks of every month.
  2. Payments are monthly, including any in month events requiring extra payment.
  3. If you wish to begin using the daycare or tutoring service, please do so two weeks before the month you wish to begin. The same rules apply to termination of services.
  4. Any incomplete payment during termination of services will be paid during that time.
  5. Any medical issues the child or student might have, please inform the staff during registration.
  6. Please keep in mind the cleanliness of the waiting area, daycare area and classrooms.
  7. Any damage caused to the equipment shall be paid for by the responsible party. Warnings may be issued in more serious cases.
  8. The fees of every month will remain the same unless :
    1. Registration fees for the first payment.
    2. Additional events are to be held in the month.
    3. Additional classes are added to the student's schedule.

Daycare Rules :

  1. Parents or guardians are to drop their child or children off on time and not any earlier.
  2. When picking up their child, parents or guardians will have a grace period of 30 minutes before they will be called by our staff.
  3. In case of emergency leave, parents or guardians are to call any active daycare staff ahead of time before picking them up. Staff's schedules will be provided on our website.
  4. Only the child's parents are allowed to pick them up. In case of a change of person, parents or guardians are to call ahead or stay to inform the staff during drop off.
  5. Lunch and tea time will be provided.
  6. When in Daycare Session :
    1. Be respectful and kind to the staff and others.
    2. No fighting or bullying.
    3. No stealing.
    4. No vandalism.

Tutoring Rules :

  1. Students are expected to arrive a minimum of 10 before the tutoring session begins. If students are late, any reasonable punishment can be given by their tutor.
  2. Any notes or references provided by the tutor are already paid for in the monthly payment.
  3. Students are not advised to stay too long after the torturing session or after 10:00 p.m., please inform the staff if students are unable to contact their parents or guardians.
  4. In case of emergency leave, parents or guardians are to call the tutor ahead of time before picking them up. Tutor's schedules will be provided on our website.
  5. Students are advised to eat lunch or dinner before tutoring sessions as food is not allowed in the classroom.
  6. Tutoring sessions are subject to change if or when any of the tutors are unavailable during a particular date. This also applies for extra classes if the tutor is behind, no additional changes acquired.
  7. When in Tutoring Session :
    1. Be respectful and kind to the staff and others.
    2. Electronic devices must be silenced or shut off.
    3. No fighting or bullying.
    4. No stealing.
    5. No vandalism.
    6. No smoking.
